Game Rules

These are the rules for this Blackjack game, you will start with $100(not real money). The minimum bet you can put in is $25, however you are always allowed to bet more than that if you're feeling lucky. You are presented with two buttons below, one button says "Start" game and the other button says "How To Play." In order to start the game click the start game portion and you will begin the main application. On the main application you will be presented with your current amount of money you have to bet and two buttons to the side that will say "Hit" and "Stay." click these respectively depending on what you want to do in the moment where you draw the cards. You will also have a little text box where you can enter how much you want to bet on this hand(remember minimum bet is $25). The currency is used as a score and will be saved once you end the game and presented only if you have a high score. You will also have a button in the bottom right hand corner saying "End Game" click on this when you think your done and want to save your highscore. If you do not know how to play click on the how to play button, it will redirect you to another page with a vidoe on how to play blackjack and display the "book" for blackjack.
